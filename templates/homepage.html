Recyclable waste

Recyclable waste is material that can be collected, sorted, processed, and remanufactured into new products.

Residual waste

Residual waste, also known as general waste, is the portion of waste that remains after all recycling, reuse, and other waste diversion strategies have been exhausted.

Food waste

Food waste, in simple terms, is food that is produced but ultimately not eaten and becomes waste.
